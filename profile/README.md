Nobo is a premier food delivery platform dedicated to connecting customers, merchants, and independent couriers across Kenya. Founded in Nairobi in 2025, we are transforming the on-demand economy through high-concurrency distributed systems and real-time logistics optimization.

---

###  Our Mission
To provide a secure, user-friendly, and efficient delivery experience that empowers local businesses, supports independent couriers, and enhances the daily lives of Kenyans through reliable, tech-driven service.

###  The Nobo Tech Stack
We build for "The Silicon Savannah," ensuring our apps perform under varying network conditions and high-demand urban surges.

* **Mobile:** Hybrid & Native architectures (Flutter/Kotlin/Swift) optimized for low-latency real-time tracking.
* **Backend:** Event-driven microservices (Go, Node.js) powered by **PostgreSQL** and **Redis**.
* **Logistics Engine:** Custom routing algorithms for Nairobi’s unique urban and peri-urban geography.
* **Infrastructure:** Cloud-native deployment (AWS) with a focus on the **Data Protection Act (2019)** compliance for user privacy.

###  Engineering Values
Our code reflects our corporate values:
- **Customer-Centricity:** We build intuitive interfaces and transparent tracking systems.
- **Scalability:** Our architecture is designed for "measured expansion" into new Kenyan cities.
- **Integrity:** Security-first mindset. We uphold the highest standards of data protection and ethical tech.
- **Innovation:** Continuous investment in order-matching efficiency and automated dispatching.
